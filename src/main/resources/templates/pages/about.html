Lindsey denies that, but between us, we both know it's true. Soon we became friends at turned into something more. We've been together since 2013, and happily married since 2019!
Our Experiences
Upon graduating with degrees in dance performance as well as arts management, we began our profesional performing career with cruise companies such as Holland America Line, Celebrity Cruises, and Princess Cruises. We were fortunate enough to work with leaders in the entertainment industry such as Broadway composer Stephen Schwartz and talent agency RWS Entertainment Group out of New York City. We traveled the world, met many friends, and even taugh dance lessons to cruis ship guests onboard!
Where We Are Now
In 2020, we moved to San Antonio, Texas to establish our home and transition to land-based careers. Matt teachs and choreographs full time for local competitive dance studios in the area, and Lindsey manages the sam studios. Matt also founded a dance ministry called Kairos Dance that merges faith, film, and dance with the goal of bringing God back into the performing arts.
